====== Modifiers ======
Created Sunday 18 November 2018

==== Ability Modifier Chart ====
page 20

The formula for calculating ability modifiers is 

	(ability - 10) / 2 

rounded down to the nearest integer.


{{.\pasted_image.png}}


==== Proficiency Modifiers ====

* untrained (= level - 2)
* trained (= level)
* expert (= level + 1)
* master (= level + 2)
* legendary (= level + 3)
